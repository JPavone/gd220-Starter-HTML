    2. Same-sex Marriage

      3. Do you support same-sex marriage?

      Yes: President Obama supports same-sex marriage. Supporters feel that restricting marriage violates the civil rights of same-sex couples and fosters inequality based on sexual orientation.

      No: Mitt Romney opposes it and supports amending the Constitution to ban gay marriage Opponents say the institution of marriage should be protected and that altering its definition would encourage relationships that conflict with their values.

    3. GUNS

      4. Do you support restrictions on the purchase and possession of guns?

      Yes: Obama has voiced support for renewed ban on assault-type weapons but has not pushed for gun control. Supporters say that the government has the authority to manage the sale, transfer, and ownership of firearms.

      No: Mitt Romney opposes stricter gun laws. Opponents say that gun ownership is a constitutionally-protected right and assert that the sale, transfer, and ownership of firearms should not be regulated by the government.
